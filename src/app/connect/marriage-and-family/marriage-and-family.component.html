Our church values strong, Christ-centered marriages and families, and we are committed to helping every household grow in faith, love, and unity.

"But as for me and my household, we will serve the Lord." — Joshua 24:15

Marriage and family are God’s design and a reflection of His covenant love for His people. At our church, we seek to strengthen marriages and families through biblical teaching, counselling, fellowship, and prayer. We believe that when Christ is at the center of the home, love, peace, and joy overflow into every relationship. Our Marriage and Family ministry supports couples, parents, and children through workshops, family enrichment programs, and mentorship opportunities. These gatherings encourage spiritual growth, effective communication, forgiveness, and godly leadership within the home. The foundation for a godly family is found in Ephesians 5:21-33, Colossians 3:18-21, Psalm 127:1, and Proverbs 22:6.
