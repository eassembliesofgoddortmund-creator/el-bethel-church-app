On request or before marriage, our church provides Premarital Counselling to help couples build a strong foundation in Christ.

"Therefore what God has joined together, let no one separate." — Mark 10:9

Premarital counselling is designed to prepare couples for a lifelong, God-centered marriage. Through these sessions, couples are guided on communication, conflict resolution, spiritual growth, and understanding the biblical roles and responsibilities within marriage. Our goal is to help couples grow closer to each other and to God as they enter this sacred covenant. In these counselling sessions, couples are encouraged to pray together, discuss expectations, and learn practical ways to maintain love, respect, and unity. The counselling also emphasizes the importance of putting Christ at the center of the marriage, ensuring a relationship that thrives on mutual trust, faith, and forgiveness. The scriptural foundation for premarital guidance can be found in Ephesians 5:22-33, 1 Corinthians 13:4-7, Colossians 3:12-17, and Proverbs 18:22.
